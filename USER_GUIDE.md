# PeerNova User Guide

Welcome to **PeerNova**! This guide outlines the step-by-step journey for both **Students** and **Tutors** to get the most out of the platform.

---

## 📚 For Students: How to Learn

### 1. Account Creation
- Navigate to the **Sign Up** page.
- Choose the **Student** role.
- Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password) and complete the registration.

### 2. Find Your Perfect Tutor
- Use the **Explore Subjects** section or the search bar to find topics you need help with (e.g., Mathematics, React.js, Physics).
- Browse through the list of expert tutors. You can filter them by **price**, **rating**, and **subject expertise**.
- Click on a tutor's profile to read their bio, see their average rating, and check their past student reviews.

### 3. Book a Session
- Once you've found a tutor, view their **Calendar/Availability**.
- Select a time slot that works for your schedule.
- Confirm the booking. Your session will immediately appear in your **Dashboard**.

### 4. The Dashboard Experience
- Log in to your **Dashboard** to see a clear overview of your upcoming classes.
- You can view your recent activities, past sessions, and messages from your tutors.
- When a session is about to begin, the session status will change to `in_progress`, and a **Join Call** button will appear next to it.

### 5. Start Learning (1-on-1 Video)
- Click **Join Call** to enter the secure WebRTC video room.
- Engage with your tutor using crystal-clear HD video, live chat, and screen sharing to go over complex problems together.

### 6. Review & Rate
- After the session ends, leave a star rating and a written review for your tutor. Your feedback helps the community find the best educators!

---

## 🎓 For Tutors: How to Teach and Earn

### 1. Create a Professional Profile
- Navigate to the **Sign Up** page and register as a **Tutor**.
- Build your profile: Add a professional photo, write a compelling bio, and list the exact **Subjects** you are qualified to teach.
- Set your **Hourly Rate** based on your experience and market demand.

### 2. Set Your Availability
- Navigate to your **Schedule** settings.
- Open up time slots in your calendar when you are free to teach. Students can only book you during these specified windows.

### 3. Accept & Manage Bookings
- When a student books you, you will receive a notification, and the session will appear on your **Dashboard** under *Upcoming Sessions*.
- You have full control over your schedule and can prepare any necessary materials before the call based on the student's needs.

### 4. Host the Session
- Log in to your **Dashboard** shortly before the scheduled time.
- When it's time to teach, the **Join Call** button will activate.
- Enter the video room, greet your student, and use the built-in communication tools (video, chat, screen share) to deliver a world-class lesson.

### 5. Get Paid & Build Your Reputation
- Once the session concludes, it is marked as completed, and the payment process is initiated securely.
- Encourage your student to leave a review. Higher ratings and positive reviews will boost your visibility in search results, helping you attract more students!
